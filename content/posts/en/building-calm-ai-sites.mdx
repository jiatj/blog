A personal site that lasts should solve publishing first, not self-promotion first.

## Why the first version must stay small

If blog pages, tool pages, About, SEO, and localization are not steady yet, adding more systems only expands the maintenance surface.

## What V1 should optimize for

- File-driven content
- Clear routing
- Stable page templates
- Good reading rhythm
- Easy iteration later

## Closing thought

The real test of V1 is not whether it looks full. It is whether it can carry your ongoing work.
